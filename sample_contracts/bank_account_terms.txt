NOVA BANK — PREMIER CURRENT ACCOUNT
TERMS AND CONDITIONS (Version 4.2 — Effective March 2024)

Thank you for opening a Premier Current Account with Nova Bank. Please read the
following terms and conditions carefully before using your account.

1. ACCOUNT FEES AND CHARGES
A monthly maintenance fee of €14.99 will be automatically debited from your account.
An annual account review fee of €25 applies each January. These fees are non-refundable
under all circumstances, including account closure.

2. OVERDRAFT FACILITY
An overdraft facility of up to €2,000 is available subject to credit assessment. An
interest rate of 22.9% APR applies to all overdrawn balances. An overdraft usage fee
of €6 per day is charged for each day the account remains overdrawn, in addition to
accrued interest. Nova Bank reserves the right to withdraw or reduce the overdraft
facility without prior notice.

3. AUTOMATIC RENEWAL AND CONTRACT CONTINUATION
This account agreement automatically renews on an annual basis. If you wish to close
your account, you must provide 30 days written notice and pay any outstanding fees.
Failure to provide notice results in automatic renewal and continued fee obligations.
Early cancellation fees of €75 apply if the account is closed within the first 12 months.

4. VARIABLE INTEREST RATES
All interest rates stated in this agreement are variable and may change at any time
without prior notice. Nova Bank reserves the right to unilaterally modify any interest
rate or fee schedule. Continued use of the account after a rate change constitutes
acceptance of the new terms.

5. MANDATORY ARBITRATION AND CLASS ACTION WAIVER
All disputes between you and Nova Bank must be resolved through binding arbitration.
You expressly waive the right to bring or participate in any class action lawsuit
against Nova Bank. Arbitration shall take place in Luxembourg and be conducted in
English. You waive the right to a jury trial or any judicial review of arbitration
decisions. Each party bears its own arbitration costs.

6. ACCOUNT CLOSURE FEES
If you close your account, a processing fee of €50 will be charged. All monthly fees
paid prior to closure are non-refundable. If your account is overdrawn at the time of
closure, the full outstanding balance plus applicable interest and penalties is due
immediately. Nova Bank may refer unpaid balances to a collections agency.

7. DATA SHARING AND THIRD-PARTY PARTNERS
By accepting these terms, you consent to Nova Bank sharing your financial data,
transaction history, and personal information with third-party partners, including
marketing companies and credit agencies, for purposes including targeted advertising
and credit profiling. You may opt out by visiting a branch in person.

8. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFER AND PROCESSING FEES
International wire transfers are subject to a processing fee of €30 plus 0.8% of the
transaction amount. SEPA transfers within the EU incur an administrative fee of €2.50
per transaction. Foreign currency exchange fees of 2.5% above the mid-market rate apply.

9. CARD AND TRANSACTION SECURITY
You are solely responsible for all transactions made with your debit card, including
unauthorized transactions. Unauthorized transactions must be reported within 30 days
of the statement date; failure to do so constitutes acceptance of the charge. In cases
of suspected negligence, you may be held liable for unlimited amounts, including
fraudulent transactions facilitated by your failure to protect your credentials.

10. CREDIT CHECKS AND ACCOUNT MODIFICATION
Continued use of this account is subject to periodic credit checks at Nova Bank's
discretion. Based on credit assessment results, Nova Bank reserves the right to modify
your account terms, reduce credit limits, increase fees, or close your account with
14 days notice.

11. MINIMUM BALANCE AND SERVICE CHARGE
A minimum balance of €500 is required to waive the monthly maintenance fee. If your
balance falls below this threshold, the full monthly service charge applies. A low
balance notification fee of €2 per alert will be charged if you enroll in balance alerts.

12. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION
