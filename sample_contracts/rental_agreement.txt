RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 1, 2024, between
Green Oak Properties B.V. ("Landlord") and the undersigned tenant ("Tenant").

1. TERM
The tenancy commences on February 1, 2024 and ends on January 31, 2025 (12 months).

2. RENT
The monthly rent is €1,350.00, due on the first calendar day of each month. A late fee
of €75 will be charged for payments received after the 5th day of the month. Rent may be
adjusted annually by the Landlord with 30 days written notice.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT
Tenant shall pay a non-refundable administrative deposit of €500 upon signing. An
additional refundable security deposit of €2,700 (two months' rent) is also required.
The administrative deposit is forfeited under all circumstances and covers agency and
administration costs.

4. AUTOMATIC RENEWAL
This lease will automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless either party
provides 60 days written notice of termination prior to the end of the current term.
Renewal rates are subject to change without prior notice.

5. EARLY TERMINATION
If Tenant terminates this lease before the agreed end date, Tenant shall pay a penalty
equal to three months' rent as liquidated damages, plus any outstanding rent through the
notice period. This penalty applies regardless of circumstances, including job relocation
or medical reasons.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
Tenant is responsible for all minor maintenance and repairs costing under €200. Landlord
reserves the right to charge Tenant for any repairs caused by Tenant's misuse. A deposit
inspection will be conducted at move-out.

7. SUBLETTING AND GUESTS
Tenant may not sublet or assign this agreement without prior written consent from Landlord.
Guests staying longer than 14 consecutive days require written approval.

8. ARBITRATION AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising under this agreement shall be resolved exclusively through binding
arbitration administered by the Dutch Arbitration Institute. Tenant expressly waives the
right to bring any claim in a court of law or to participate in any class action. The cost
of arbitration shall be borne equally by both parties.

9. INDEMNIFICATION AND LIABILITY
Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from any and all claims, damages, or
expenses arising from Tenant's use of the premises, including claims by third parties.
Landlord's total liability under this agreement is limited to one month's rent under all
circumstances.

10. PERSONAL GUARANTEE
All financial obligations of the Tenant under this agreement are personally guaranteed.
In the event of default or breach, Landlord may pursue Tenant's personal assets,
including bank accounts and future earnings, to satisfy any outstanding amounts.

11. UTILITIES AND SERVICE CHARGES
Tenant is responsible for electricity, gas, water, and internet. An administrative fee
of €30 per month will be charged for utility bill management and forwarding services,
payable even if Tenant manages utilities directly.

12. NOTICE REQUIREMENTS
Either party must provide a minimum of 60 days written notice before termination or
non-renewal. Notice must be sent by registered post to the addresses stated in this
agreement. Email notice is not accepted.

13. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement is governed exclusively by the laws of the Netherlands. Any legal
proceedings must be initiated in Amsterdam courts. Tenant waives the right to bring
proceedings in any other jurisdiction.

14. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Any verbal
representations made by the Landlord or its agents are not binding.
